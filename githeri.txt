it's a traditional dish in my country ie in Kenya
basically a mixture of beans and maize.
preparation
	1. Have the beans clean ready and the maize seeds separate from the cob.
	2. Prepare a pot by filling it with water 
	3. Add the mixture of the two ie maize and beans into the pot after clean up.
	4. Place the port on fire mostly the preferred choice is wood since it takes a while and has cost benefits.
	5. Once the beans and maize are ready add salt done. 
	6. You have a meal.
serve
